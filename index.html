Biography

I am an Algorithm Engineer at Alibaba DAMO Academy, Hangzhou, China. Before that, I got my Ph.D. and BS degrees both from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. My Ph.D. advisor is Prof. Albert Chi Shing CHUNG. My research interests are in medical image registration, medical image analysis and deep learning.
