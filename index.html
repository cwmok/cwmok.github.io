Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Albert Chi Shing CHUNG. Before that, I received a Bachelor of Engineering degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2017. My research interests are in medical image registration, medical image analysis and deep learning.
